    So, you want to know about Allen Henderson?

    I am a 22 year old guy that loves to make websites for people. If you are still not convinced that this is what I love to do, then you may ask all my teachers from middle and high school. They will tell you the same thing.

    Let me tell you how web developing all started to become my field of interest

    When I was much younger, I loved playing on the computer and using the internet. I finally figured out that people, my age, were creating websites. I found online website builders. However, those website builders had these plans that were ridiculous. What I mean by that is, their plans were like cell phone company plans. Then I finally figured out you can code them yourself. My first though was, "I know I can learn that." When I was in high school, I took computer classes all throughout high school. I loved it. There was this one class called "Multimedia Publishing" where we pretty much did a newscast. I was having so much fun with it. I realized that I loved web development. With that being said, I am going back to school to get my certification in it.

    In the meantime, I am also planning on making more websites from scratch to help build my portfolio. With that being said, if you do not see what you are looking for, I am most likely either planning on it or already working on that template.
